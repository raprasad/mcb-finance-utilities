Expense Code Entry

  • Expense codes may be entered in any format, including columns copied out of Excel.
  • Examples of valid formats:
    • '-' separator:   370-31330-8550-512701-600200-0000-50699
    • '.' separator:   370.31330.8550.512701.600200.0000.50699
    • no separator:   370313308550512701600200000050699
    • 'xxxx' object code:   370-31330-xxxx-512701-600200-0000-50699
      • changes 'xxxx' to '{{ SUBSTITUTE_OBJECT_CODE }}'
      • works with any separator--or no separator
    • big mess:   370-31330*8550/512701-.--600200.000050699
  • Blank spaces and line breaks are ignored. Invalid codes (or words, etc) are simply marked as invalid.
  • Validates up to {{ MAX_EXPENSE_CODES_TO_VALIDATE }} expense codes at a time. Additional codes are ignored.
